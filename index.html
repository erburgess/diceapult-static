Meet the Designers

The creative duo behind the mayhem

üë§

Chad Smith

Lead Designer

Senior Special Effects Designer at Walt Disney Imagineering for over a decade. Chad designed the unique Diceapult catapult mechanism that makes the game a true hands-on experience.

At Disney, Chad created special effects for some of the most beloved attractions in the world: Seven Dwarfs Mine Train, Frozen Ever After, Avatar Flight of Passage, Mickey & Minnie's Runaway Railway, Star Wars: Rise of the Resistance, and Tiana's Bayou Adventure.

He holds a US Patent for visual effect systems and worked on special projects for Imagineering's Blue Sky and R&D departments. Currently Head of Technology at the Webb Schools in Southern California, Chad brings world-class theme park engineering to the tabletop.

E.R. Burgess

Co-Designer & Developer

Disney Interactive veteran who wrote and produced the multi-million-selling Who Wants to be a Millionaire video games. Over 40 years in tabletop gaming as designer, developer, and consultant.

At Disney, E.R. wrote dialogue and requirements for over 20 products and introduced Disney's first-ever downloadable content. He hosted the award-winning Board Game Babylon podcast for a decade and has been referenced in academic studies of games.

As a tabletop game consultant, he's worked with Academy Games, Fowers Games, WizKids, Sunrise Tornado, and more on campaign design, story development, rules editing, and crowdfunding management. Author of two novels, and the upcoming books Optional Rules: Variant for Life I Learned From Board Games and Board Game Babble-On: Stories from 45 Years of Adventures on the Tabletop.
